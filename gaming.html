Mobile Gaming Event

In the mobile gaming event, each school can send four students (one squad) to compete. The featured game is BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile), where players will engage in intense mobile battles. The event highlights participants' skills in strategy, quick decision-making. Players must communicate effectively and adapt to challenges during the game in order to succeed. It is a competitive and exciting opportunity for students to showcase their gaming talents, organized by Ryan International School, Vasant Kunj.

Event Rules and Guidelines

  • Cheating, hacking, or exploiting game mechanics is strictly prohibited.
  • Allowed devices: mobile phones, tablets, headsets, earbuds.
  • Prohibited items: finger gloves, laptops, triggers, or any external device enhancing gameplay.
  • Participants must ensure their equipment is functional. Maintain clear and respectful communication.
  • Technical/software issues are the participant's responsibility.
  • Devices should be charged; no Wi-Fi will be provided, so bring your own data.
  • Rule violations will lead to disqualification.
